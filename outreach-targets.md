## Outreach Tips

1. **Always use the official partnership/vendor application form** when one exists -- cold emails to generic addresses have low conversion.
2. **For brokerages and associations**, lead with ROI: "Reduce contract review time by X%, catch Y% more risk clauses."
3. **For HR platforms**, position as a marketplace integration: "Add contract analysis to your existing workflow."
4. **For directories**, the free listing is usually enough to start; upgrade to paid after you have reviews.
5. **For VCs/accelerators**, apply through official channels; warm intros via LinkedIn are more effective than cold email.
6. **For construction**, emphasize compliance and change-order risk -- that is where the pain is.
